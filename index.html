Understanding the Deceptive Design feedback loop (& what can designers do about it)

By Al Weiner & Michael Park

Advised by Leah Plunkett and Harry Brignull

Our team of technology designers and a legal expert from Harvard agree that we have a duty to make the internet a place where young people can feel safe.

We wanted to understand the role that technology designers play in protecting youth from Deceptive Designs so we set out to map the system.

A toolkit of subversion emerged.
